We believe in the Worship of Water, as it is the source of all beginning, include all life, nourishes one and all continuously and sustains us. Life without water is unimaginable and impossible. The Climate change scenario of India and the world remind us to arise, awake and do justice to the man kind by adopting suitable measures for the conservation of soil and water. The administrators and policy makers are looking for an ecofriendly technology to resolve the issues related to the changing environmental conditions. The scientific community and experts caution the human population to be better equipped with advanced technology, scientific approach and decision taking ability. Therefore, it is necessary to develop a human resource capability in water, soil and environmental resources management. To achieve this target, we have an excellent mix of talented young engineers/scientists and professional, highly experienced, and dedicated experts in the area of Water Resources Management, Hydrology and Hydrogeology, Soil Physics and Environmental Management.
